Welcome to Big Bang Engineering

Big Bang Engineering Company (Pvt) Ltd is a Pan African, customer centric, green energy and electrical power solutions provider.The company vision is to provide sustainable green energy solutions that enhance our lives and the environment for generations.

Big Bang Engineering comprises of competent and experienced staff managed by two highly experienced and motivated electrical engineers. Othmar Mafusire and Simbarashe Chadehumbe have a combined experience of more than 10 years in the Electrical Power Industry. Simbarashe has handled projects of varying magnitude and complexity in Zimbabwe, Namibia and Botswana. Othmar has specialised in the telecommunications sector providing technical power solutions for MNOs in Zambia and Zimbabwe.
